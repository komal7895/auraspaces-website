About Us

Founded in 2015, Elegant Spaces Interiors has grown into a trusted name in the interior design industry across India. Our journey started with a passion for transforming ordinary places into visually captivating and functional spaces.

Mission: To deliver elegant, efficient, and modern interior solutions that match our client’s needs and lifestyle.

Key Milestones:

  • 2015: Launched in Delhi with 3 passionate designers.
  • 2018: Completed 100+ residential and commercial projects.
  • 2023: Awarded “Top 10 Interior Firms in North India”

Our Team: Led by founder and chief designer Aarti Mehra, our talented team includes space planners, architects, and Vastu consultants.
